
### [Rubric/Overall-5] 整体 — 5分（Native-like）
描述：Native-like facility in the use of language with syntactic variety, appropriate word choice and phrases; well-controlled text organization; precise use of grammar and conventions; rare language inaccuracies that do not impede communication.
适用：全文无明显中式直译；长难句结构完整且多样；主谓一致、时态、冠词使用几乎无懈可击；段落衔接自然流畅。
对标参考：IELTS Writing Task 2 Band 9；CET-4/6 14分档（13-15分）[reference:7]

### [Rubric/Overall-3] 整体 — 3分（Intermediate）
描述：Facility limited to the use of common structures and generic vocabulary; organization generally controlled although connection sometimes absent or unsuccessful; errors in grammar, syntax, and usage are present; communication is impeded by language inaccuracies in some cases.
适用：作文中出现“there have”、“open the light”等显眼中式搭配；简单句堆砌，复杂从句尝试时频繁出错（如缺连接词、语序混乱）；主谓一致和三单后缀漏加问题集中。
对标参考：IELTS Writing Task 2 Band 7；CET-4/6 11分档（10-12分）[reference:8]

### [Rubric/Overall-1] 整体 — 1分（Novice）
描述：A limited range of familiar words or phrases loosely strung together; frequent errors in grammar and syntax; no clear organization; communication impeded in most cases.
适用：严重跑题，段落划分缺失；句子碎片化（缺主语或谓语）；基础时态（一般现在/过去）混用严重；冠词完全缺失（如“She is teacher”）。
对标参考：IELTS Writing Task 2 Band 4；CET-4/6 5分档（4-6分）[reference:9]



### [Rubric/Cohesion-5] 连贯与衔接 — 5分
描述：Text organization consistently well controlled using a variety of effective linguistic features such as reference (pronouns/demonstratives) and transitional words/phrases to connect ideas across sentences and paragraphs; appropriate overlap of ideas.
适用：段落内句间指代明确（如使用“this”、“these”、“such”回扣前句）；递进（furthermore）、转折（nevertheless）、因果（consequently）等连接词使用精准且不机械重复。
对标参考：IELTS Band 9 Coherence & Cohesion；CET-4/6 14分档（13-15分）[reference:10]

### [Rubric/Cohesion-3] 连贯与衔接 — 3分
描述：Organization generally controlled; cohesive devices used but limited in type (mainly basic conjunctions); some repetitive, mechanical, or faulty use of cohesion within and/or between sentences; some unsuccessful connections.
适用：大量重复“and”、“but”、“so”连接句子；缺少“however”、“therefore”等逻辑过渡词；指代词（it/they/this）指向模糊，导致读起来跳跃感强。
对标参考：IELTS Band 7 Coherence & Cohesion；CET-4/6 11分档（10-12分）[reference:11]

### [Rubric/Cohesion-1] 连贯与衔接 — 1分
描述：No clear control of organization; cohesive devices not present or unsuccessfully used; presentation of ideas unclear; ideas are discernible but difficult to relate to each other.
适用：全文无分段或分段混乱；无任何连接词或过渡语；句子之间观点毫无逻辑关联，像是孤立的句子拼凑。
对标参考：IELTS Band 4 Coherence & Cohesion；CET-4/6 5分档（4-6分）[reference:12]


### [Rubric/Syntax-5] 句法 — 5分
描述：Flexible and effective use of a full range of syntactic structures including simple, compound, and complex sentences; there may be rare minor and negligible errors in sentence formation.
适用：熟练交替使用简单句、并列句和各类复杂从句（定语、状语、名词性从句）；语序完全符合英语习惯；长短句结合疏密有致。
对标参考：IELTS Band 9 Grammatical Range；CET-4/6 14分档（13-15分）[reference:13]

### [Rubric/Syntax-3] 句法 — 3分
描述：Simple, compound, and complex syntactic structures present, although the range may be limited; some apparent errors in sentence formation, especially in more complex sentences.
适用：简单句占绝对主导；尝试写“which / because / although”从句时经常出现结构残缺（缺主语或缺谓语）；有中式语序痕迹（如时间地点状语位置不当）。
对标参考：IELTS Band 7 Grammatical Range；CET-4/6 11分档（10-12分）[reference:14]

### [Rubric/Syntax-1] 句法 — 1分
描述：Pervasive and basic errors in sentence structure and word order that cause confusion; basic sentence errors common.
适用：大量句子碎片（如“Because I like.”）和逗号拼接句（如“I like reading, I read books.”）；词序严重混乱，无法构建完整独立分句。
对标参考：IELTS Band 4 Grammatical Range；CET-4/6 5分档（4-6分）[reference:15]


### [Rubric/Vocab-5] 词汇 — 5分
描述：Wide range of vocabulary flexibly and effectively used to convey precise meanings; skillful use of topic-related terms and less common words; rare negligible inaccuracies in word use.
适用：同义词替换丰富（如交替使用“demonstrate / indicate / reveal”）；用词精准地道，无“learn knowledge”、“make a research”等中式搭配。
对标参考：IELTS Band 9 Lexical Resource；CET-4/6 14分档（13-15分）[reference:16]

### [Rubric/Vocab-3] 词汇 — 3分
描述：Minimally adequate range of vocabulary for the topic; no precise use of subtle word meanings; topic-related terms only used occasionally; attempts to use less common vocabulary but with some inaccuracy.
适用：大量重复使用“good / bad / important / people”等基础词汇；尝试使用高级词时混淆词性（如用“economy”当形容词）；拼写错误开始干扰理解（如“goverment”）。
对标参考：IELTS Band 7 Lexical Resource；CET-4/6 11分档（10-12分）[reference:17]

### [Rubric/Vocab-1] 词汇 — 1分
描述：Limited vocabulary often inappropriately used; limited control of word choice and word forms; little attempt to use topic-related terms.
适用：词不达意，频繁直译中文含义；无法区分“affect/effect”、“rise/raise”等易混词；单词拼写严重错误导致语义扭曲。
对标参考：IELTS Band 4 Lexical Resource；CET-4/6 5分档（4-6分）[reference:18]


### [Rubric/Phraseology-5] 短语搭配 — 5分
描述：Flexible and effective use of a variety of phrases, such as idioms, collocations, and lexical bundles, to convey precise and subtle meanings; rare minor inaccuracies that are negligible.
适用：地道使用学术书面搭配（如“take into consideration”、“in light of”、“play a crucial role”）；无中文生硬拼接短语。
对标参考：IELTS Band 9 Lexical Resource (Collocation)；CET-4/6 14分档（13-15分）[reference:19]

### [Rubric/Phraseology-3] 短语搭配 — 3分
描述：Evident use of phrases such as idioms, collocations, and lexical bundles but without much variety; some noticeable repetitions and misuses; some errors in collocation may appear.
适用：固定搭配储备有限，反复使用“in my opinion”、“on the other hand”等模板套语；出现“discuss about”（应为discuss）、“depend with”（应为depend on）等介词搭配错误。
对标参考：IELTS Band 7 Lexical Resource (Collocation)；CET-4/6 11分档（10-12分）[reference:20]

### [Rubric/Phraseology-1] 短语搭配 — 1分
描述：Memorized chunks of language, or simple phrasal patterns predominate; many repetitions and misuses of phrases; little ability to generate original appropriate collocations.
适用：完全按中文字面组合短语（如“to solve problem”缺冠词）；只能使用“How are you”级别的背诵整句，无法灵活拆解重组搭配。
对标参考：IELTS Band 4 Lexical Resource (Collocation)；CET-4/6 5分档（4-6分）[reference:21]


### [Rubric/Grammar-5] 语法 — 5分
描述：Command of grammar and usage with few or no errors; grammatical morphology (third person, plural, possessive) and word classes (prepositions, pronouns, nouns, verbs) are used accurately throughout.
适用：全文三单后缀（-s）、名词复数（-s/-es）、物主代词（its/their）全部正确；时态上下文统一（全篇现在时或全篇过去时清晰一致）。
对标参考：IELTS Band 9 Grammatical Accuracy；CET-4/6 14分档（13-15分）[reference:22]

### [Rubric/Grammar-3] 语法 — 3分
描述：Some errors in grammar and usage; errors may be frequent and cause some difficulty for the reader; accuracy is lower in complex structures than in simple ones.
适用：主谓不一致（如“He have”、“The study suggest”）在文中多次出现；一般过去时与现在时在同一段落内毫无理由地切换；冠词（a/an/the）系统性缺失或滥用。
对标参考：IELTS Band 7 Grammatical Accuracy；CET-4/6 11分档（10-12分）[reference:23]

### [Rubric/Grammar-1] 语法 — 1分
描述：Errors in grammar and usage throughout; grammatical errors are frequent and may impede meaning; punctuation is often faulty or inadequate.
适用：时态、人称、名词单复数全程混乱；句子缺少谓语动词；介词（in/on/at）完全随机使用，导致无法理解时间地点关系。
对标参考：IELTS Band 4 Grammatical Accuracy；CET-4/6 5分档（4-6分）[reference:24]


### [Rubric/Conventions-5] 格式规范 — 5分
描述：Consistent use of appropriate conventions to convey meaning; spelling, capitalization, and punctuation errors nonexistent or negligible.
适用：拼写零错误；句首字母均大写；逗号、句号、撇号（如“it's”与“its”）使用精准；段落缩进或空行标准统一。
对标参考：IELTS Band 9 Conventions；CET-4/6 14分档（13-15分）[reference:25]

### [Rubric/Conventions-3] 格式规范 — 3分
描述：Developing use of conventions to convey meaning; errors in spelling, capitalization, and punctuation that are sometimes distracting.
适用：常见单词拼写错误（如“becuase”、“teached”）；句首小写频繁；滥用逗号连接多个完整句子（run-on sentences），偶尔分散读者注意力。
对标参考：IELTS Band 7 Conventions；CET-4/6 11分档（10-12分）[reference:26]

### [Rubric/Conventions-1] 格式规范 — 1分
描述：Minimal use of conventions; spelling, capitalization, and punctuation errors throughout; presentation of ideas unclear.
适用：全篇无标点或乱用标点；大量拼写错误导致单词无法识别（如“enviroment”、“goverment”）；无句首大写，全文一个小写字母连着写；无段落划分。
对标参考：IELTS Band 4 Conventions；CET-4/6 5分档（4-6分）[reference:27]
