### [Grammar/主谓一致] Subject–Verb Agreement
说明:单数主语（he/she/it 或单数名词）后，动词一般加 -s；不定代词（each、everyone）作主语时谓语也用单数。中国学习者受汉语无形态变化影响，常遗漏。
错误例:He have great grades. / My mom talk with him. / Each of the students have a book.
正确例:He has great grades. / My mom talked with him. / Each of the students has a book.
触发线索:he/she/it + 动词原形；单数名词 + 复数动词；each/everyone + have/are/do

### [Grammar/时态混乱] Tense Confusion
说明:最常见的是过去时与现在时混用（如用 go 描述昨天的事）；此外，间接引语中 said + will → would，时间状语从句用现在时表将来。中国学习者受汉语无时态变化影响。
错误例:I go to school yesterday. / She said she will come. / I will call you when I will arrive.
正确例:I went to school yesterday. / She said she would come. / I will call you when I arrive.
触发线索:yesterday/last week + 现在时动词；said/told + that + will/can；when/as soon as/before 从句中出现 will

### [Grammar/动词名词化过度] Unnecessary Verb + Noun
说明:优先用动词本身，而非"make/give/have + 名词"。中国学习者受汉语"做出改进"、"进行讨论"影响。
错误例:We must make an improvement in our work.
正确例:We must improve our work.
触发线索:make/give/have + improvement/discussion/decision/contribution/reference

### [Grammar/名词堆砌] The Noun Plague
说明:能用动词说清的，不要绕成"名词 + of + 动作名词"。中国学习者受汉语"……的方法"影响。
错误例:This is a method of evaluation of the data.
正确例:This is a method of evaluating the data.
触发线索:method/way/approach + of + evaluation/implementation/analysis

### [Grammar/被动语态滥用] Passive Voice Overuse
说明:主动语态通常更直接。中国学习者受汉语"被"字句影响，过度使用被动。
错误例:The problem should be considered by us.
正确例:We should consider the problem.
触发线索:be + 过去分词 + by + 施动者（尤其是 us/them/the government）

### [Grammar/近义词堆叠] Redundant Twins
说明:汉语用成对近义词加强语气（如"观点意见"），英语选一个即可。
错误例:We should listen to the views and opinions of the public.
正确例:We should listen to the views of the public.
触发线索:views and opinions / help and assistance / problems and difficulties（and 连接两个近义词）

### [Grammar/重复指代与重复陈述] Repeated References and Repetitive Statements
说明:同一段落中反复变换说法指代同一事物，或用不同句子重复同一信息，英语中应避免。
错误例:The environment is important. This problem affects everyone. / China has a large population. It is a country with many people.
正确例:The environment is important and affects everyone. / China has a large population.
触发线索:同一段落中 this problem / this issue / this matter 交替出现；相邻两句表达同一核心信息

### [Grammar/逗号拼接] Comma Splice
说明:两个完整句子不能用逗号直接连接，需用句号、分号或连接词。中国学习者受汉语"一逗到底"影响。
错误例:I like reading, I read books every day.
正确例:I like reading. I read books every day.
触发线索:两个完整句子（主谓齐全）仅用逗号连接，无连接词

### [Grammar/句子残缺] Sentence Fragment
说明:从属分句不能单独成句，必须有主句。
错误例:Because I was late.
正确例:Because I was late, I missed the bus.
触发线索:Because/Although/If/When + 句子后直接句号结尾，无主句

### [Grammar/介词误用] Preposition Misuse
说明:及物动词后不加介词；不及物动词需加正确介词；不按汉语逻辑套用介词。
错误例:We discussed about the problem. / I listened the music. / We should depend the government.
正确例:We discussed the problem. / I listened to the music. / We should depend on the government.
触发线索:discuss about；according to me；listen/depend + 直接宾语（缺介词）

### [Grammar/冠词缺失] Article Omission
说明:单数可数名词前一般需加冠词。汉语无冠词系统，中国学习者易遗漏。
错误例:She is teacher.
正确例:She is a teacher.
触发线索:单数可数名词前无 a/an/the（非专有名词、非复数、非不可数）

### [Grammar/可数不可数混淆] Countable vs. Uncountable Nouns
说明:英语中不可数名词（如 information、homework、advice、furniture、knowledge）不能加 -s，也不能与数词或 many 直接连用。中国学习者受汉语“可数”习惯影响，常误加复数或与 many 搭配。
错误例:I have many homeworks to do. / Can you give me some advices? / We need more furnitures.
正确例:I have much homework to do. / Can you give me some advice? / We need more furniture.
触发线索:homeworks / advices / informations / furnitures / knowledges（不可数名词 + -s）；many + 不可数名词

### [Grammar/空洞名词冗余] Empty Nouns (Category + Abstract)
说明:范畴词（problem/issue/field）和抽象名词（pace/scale/scope）常不增加语义，应删去。中国学习者受汉语"……的问题"、"……的步伐"影响。
错误例:We must solve the problem of pollution. / We must accelerate the pace of reform.
正确例:We must solve pollution. / We must accelerate reform.
触发线索:the problem/issue + of；the pace/scale/scope/process/level + of；in the field/area + of

### [Grammar/双主语] Double Subject
说明:英语一个简单句只能有一个主语。中国学习者受汉语"话题—评论"结构影响，先说一个名词，再用代词重复指代。
错误例:China, it is a large country.
正确例:China is a large country.
触发线索:句首名词短语 + 逗号 + it/they + 谓语（删除 it/they 后句子仍然成立）

### [Grammar/垂悬修饰语] Dangling Modifiers
说明:-ing/-ed 短语或不定式短语的逻辑主语须与句子主语一致。
错误例:Looking out of the window, the mountain was beautiful.
正确例:Looking out of the window, I saw a beautiful mountain.
触发线索:-ing/-ed 短语位于句首，后接主句的主语不是该动作的执行者

### [Grammar/冗长there be] Wordy "There be"
说明:直接用主语+谓语比"There be + 名词 + 定语从句"更简洁。
错误例:There are many factors that influence this phenomenon.
正确例:Many factors influence this phenomenon.
触发线索:There are + 复数名词 + that/who 定语从句

### [Grammar/连词冗余] Redundant Conjunctions
说明:because 和 so 不能同时使用；although 和 but 不能同时使用。中国学习者受汉语"因为……所以……"、"虽然……但是……"结构影响。
错误例:Because he was tired, so he went to bed early.
正确例:Because he was tired, he went to bed early.
触发线索:because 和 so 在同一句；although/though 和 but 在同一句

### [Grammar/频率副词错位] Misplaced Adverb of Frequency
说明:频率副词（always, often, sometimes）应放在动词前、be 动词后。
错误例:I every day go to school. / She is late always.
正确例:I go to school every day. / She is always late.
触发线索:every day/always + 主语 + 动词（时间状语夹在主语和动词之间）；主语 + be + 频率副词句末

### [Grammar/比较级错误] Incorrect Comparatives
说明:两类常见错误：一是单音节词加 more（如 more bigger），二是比较对象不对等（如拿北京天气和上海比较）。
错误例:This city is more bigger than that one. / The weather in Beijing is colder than Shanghai.
正确例:This city is bigger than that one. / The weather in Beijing is colder than that in Shanghai.
触发线索:more + 单音节形容词（big/tall/small）；than 前后比较对象不对等

### [Grammar/双重否定] Double Negation
说明:一个句子只能有一个否定词。中国学习者受汉语"双重否定表强调"影响。
错误例:I don't have nothing.
正确例:I don't have anything.
触发线索:not/n't + no/nobody/nothing/never 在同一句

### [Grammar/时间状语错位] Misplaced Time Adverbial
说明:具体时间点通常置于句末，真正的问题是夹在主语和动词之间。
错误例:I yesterday went to the library.
正确例:I went to the library yesterday.
触发线索:yesterday/last week/at 8 o'clock 夹在主语和动词之间

### [Grammar/程度副词修饰动词] Misplaced Adverb of Degree
说明:very 修饰形容词/副词，不可修饰动词。中国学习者受汉语"非常喜欢"影响。
错误例:I very like reading books.
正确例:I like reading books very much.
触发线索:very + like/enjoy/want/hope

### [Grammar/代词指代不明] Unclear Pronoun Reference
说明:it/they/this 须明确指代前文某个名词，不能让读者猜。
错误例:The government announced its decision, but they later changed it.
正确例:The government announced its decision, but later changed it.
触发线索:they/them/their 出现，但前文有两个或以上可能的先行词（如 government 和 committee），读者无法确定指代哪一个

### [Grammar/代词单复数不一致] Pronoun-Antecedent Agreement
说明:单数主语不能用复数代词指代。
错误例:Everyone should do their homework.
正确例:Everyone should do his or her homework.
触发线索:everyone/everybody/anyone/each + their/they/them

### [Grammar/修饰语冗余] Unnecessary Modifiers
说明:修饰语含义已被名词包含时删去。
错误例:We need mutual cooperation.
正确例:We need cooperation.
触发线索:mutual + cooperation/help/understanding；future + prospects/plan；past + history；final + outcome

### [Grammar/平行结构错误] Faulty Parallelism
说明:列举时各项须用相同语法形式，不混用不定式、动名词、名词。
错误例:I like swimming, to run, and reading.
正确例:I like swimming, running, and reading.
触发线索:and/or 连接的两项及以上，语法形式不一致（如 -ing 与 to 混用）
