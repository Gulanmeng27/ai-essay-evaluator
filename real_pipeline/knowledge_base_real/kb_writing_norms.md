
### [Norm/整体结构] Introduction-Body-Conclusion Structure 引言-主体-结论结构
说明:议论文应遵循“引言—主体—结论”三段式结构。引言提出背景和论点，主体分段展开论证，结论总结观点并升华。
为什么:清晰的三段式结构让读者快速把握文章脉络，便于评分者按“组织结构”维度给分。
正例:"This essay argues that social media has a positive impact on youth. First, it enhances communication. Second, it provides learning opportunities. In conclusion, social media, when used properly, benefits young people."

### [Norm/整体结构] Thesis Statement 总论点句
说明:引言段末尾须用一句话明确陈述全文核心论点，点明作者立场，而非仅仅介绍话题。
为什么:让读者在一开始就知道文章的中心观点，后续论证都围绕这句话展开。
正例:"Although some argue that technology isolates people, I believe it actually strengthens social connections by enabling instant communication."
反例:"This essay will discuss the advantages and disadvantages of technology."

### [Norm/结论] Summary and Final Comment 总结与升华
说明:结论段应概括主体部分的要点，并以一句有深度的评论收尾（如展望未来、提出建议或警示）。
为什么:让文章有完整的结束感，给读者留下深刻印象。
正例:"In conclusion, social media offers both opportunities and risks. Educators should embrace its potential while teaching students critical thinking skills. The future of learning lies in balance."



### [Norm/段落结构] Topic Sentence 段首主题句
说明:每段开头用一句主题句点明本段中心，后续句子围绕它展开。
为什么:让读者快速抓住段落要点，提升连贯性。
正例:"Technology has changed how teenagers socialize. First, it allows them to maintain friendships across long distances. Second, it introduces them to diverse perspectives."

### [Norm/段落结构] Paragraph Unity 段落统一性
说明:每段只讨论一个中心观点，所有句子都服务于该段的主题句。
为什么:避免段落内容混杂，保持论证清晰。
正例:"Social media improves communication. It allows instant messaging. It enables video calls. It helps people share daily updates."
反例:"Social media improves communication. It was invented in the early 2000s. Some people dislike it. It can also be used for education."

### [Norm/段落结构] Paragraph Development 段落的论证展开
说明:段落应按“主题句→证据→解释→回扣”结构展开。先亮观点，再给证据（事实、数据、例子），然后解释证据如何支持观点，最后回扣论点或过渡到下一段。
为什么:确保论证充分、逻辑完整，避免只有观点没有支撑。
正例:"Social media enhances learning. For example, students can access online courses and video tutorials. This allows them to study at their own pace and review difficult concepts repeatedly. Thus, social media serves as a valuable educational tool."
反例:"Social media enhances learning. I think it is very helpful."



### [Norm/衔接与过渡] Transitional Words — Addition 递进过渡词
说明:需要补充同类观点时，用递进类过渡词（furthermore, moreover, in addition, additionally）。避免全文反复使用 and。
为什么:递进过渡词能清晰表明“这是同一个方向的另一个观点”，增强段落内部和段落之间的逻辑。
正例:"Furthermore, social media provides access to diverse perspectives. Moreover, it facilitates collaboration among students."

### [Norm/衔接与过渡] Transitional Words — Contrast 对比过渡词
说明:需要引出对立观点或转折时，用对比类过渡词（however, nevertheless, in contrast, on the other hand）。避免用 but 连接所有转折。
为什么:对比过渡词能明确提示读者“观点即将转向”，帮助读者跟上论证逻辑。
正例:"Some argue that social media distracts students. However, when used in moderation, it can actually enhance focus by providing interactive learning materials."

### [Norm/衔接与过渡] Transitional Words — Cause and Effect 因果过渡词
说明:需要表达因果关系时，用因果类过渡词（therefore, consequently, as a result, thus）。避免简单堆砌 so 或 because。
为什么:因果过渡词能清晰展示论证链条，帮助读者理解“为什么这个观点成立”。
正例:"Students increasingly use online resources for learning. Consequently, traditional textbooks are being used less frequently in classrooms."

### [Norm/衔接与过渡] Cohesive Devices — Reference 指代衔接
说明:用代词（it, they, this, these）回指前文提到的名词，避免反复重复同一词语。
为什么:指代能增强句间衔接，使行文更流畅、更地道。
正例:"Social media has transformed communication. This platform allows users to connect instantly. It also enables sharing of ideas across borders."
反例:"Social media has transformed communication. Social media allows users to connect instantly. Social media also enables sharing of ideas."

### [Norm/衔接与过渡] Logical Connectors Between Paragraphs 段间逻辑连接
说明:在段落之间使用逻辑连接词或过渡句，明确指示段落之间的关系（递进、转折、因果、举例）。
为什么:让读者清晰感知全文论证的推进方向和整体脉络。
正例:"Having discussed the benefits of social media, this essay now turns to its potential drawbacks."



### [Norm/文体] Avoid Vague Language 避免模糊表达
说明:避免使用模糊表达（good, bad, nice, a lot, very, thing），选用更具体、更精准的词汇。
为什么:模糊表达削弱论证力度，使文章显得不专业。
正例:"Social media has a significant impact on adolescents' self-esteem."
反例:"Social media has a very big effect on young people."

### [Norm/文体] Avoid Clichés 避免陈词滥调
说明:避免使用陈词滥调（in modern society, with the development of technology, every coin has two sides, more and more）。
为什么:陈词滥调让文章缺乏新意，评分者常见此类表达，易产生审美疲劳。
正例:"The rapid advancement of digital technology has transformed education."
反例:"With the development of modern society, technology is playing a more and more important role."

### [Norm/文体] Avoid Overusing "There be" 避免滥用"There be"
说明:避免用“There is / There are”开头，改用有实际主语的句子，让表达更直接有力。
为什么:“There be”结构让句子变弱，缺乏主语会削弱表达力度。
正例:"Several factors contribute to climate change."
反例:"There are several factors that contribute to climate change."



### [Norm/用词] Choose Precise and Formal Words 精准正式选词
说明:选用正式、精准的词汇替代口语化或笼统的表达。例如：use 替代 get, investigate 替代 look into, demonstrate 替代 show；用“environmental degradation”替代“environmental problems”，用“address”替代“solve”。
为什么:精准正式的用词能提升文章的学术性和说服力。
正例:"The government should address environmental degradation."
反例:"The government should solve the problem of the environment."

### [Norm/用词] Use Synonyms to Avoid Repetition 使用同义词避免重复
说明:在同一段落或相邻段落中，交替使用同义词或近义词表达同一概念，避免反复使用同一个词。
为什么:词汇丰富度是作文评分的重要维度（Lexical Resource），重复用词会扣分。
正例:"Social media influences how people communicate. This platform shapes social interactions. Its impact extends to politics and commerce."
反例:"Social media influences communication. Social media also influences politics. Social media influences many aspects of life."

### [Norm/用词] Avoid Overgeneralization 避免绝对化
说明:避免绝对化表达（always, never, all, every），改用更谨慎的措辞（often, rarely, many, some, tends to, suggests that）。
为什么:学术写作要求表达严谨、留有余地，绝对化表述容易被反驳。
正例:"Many young people use social media daily, which suggests a significant cultural shift."
反例:"All young people use social media every day."

### [Norm/用词] Use Hedging Language 使用缓冲性语言
说明:在表达观点或解读证据时，使用缓冲性语言（suggests, indicates, appears, seems, may, could, perhaps）。
为什么:学术写作需要承认论证的局限性，避免过于武断。
正例:"These findings suggest that social media may play a role in shaping adolescent identity."
反例:"Social media shapes adolescent identity."
